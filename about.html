About Us

Cooking Since 1990

Welcome to KFC, where the legacy of Colonel Harland Sanders comes to life in every flavorful bite. With a rich history dating back to the 1930s, KFC has become an iconic global brand, celebrated for its world-famous Original Recipe chicken and commitment to serving high-quality, freshly prepared meals. Our story began with Colonel Sanders' passion for bringing people together over delicious food. Today, that same spirit lives on in our restaurants worldwide. Step into the world of KFC, where crispy, juicy chicken meets a warm and inviting atmosphere. At KFC, we take pride in offering more than just a meal; we provide an experience.

Explore our diverse menu featuring a variety of chicken options, sandwiches, and mouthwatering sides. Our commitment to quality and the Colonel's secret blend of 11 herbs and spices ensures a taste that's unmistakably KFC. As we continue to evolve and innovate, our dedication to customer satisfaction remains unwavering. Join us on a journey that combines tradition with modern flair, where each visit to KFC is a celebration of good food, good company, and the timeless joy of sharing a meal. Thank you for being a part of the KFC family. We look forward to serving you and creating delicious memories for years to come.
